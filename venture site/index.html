The World's Ultimate Incubator

HW Venture is a student-alumni entrepreneurship and innovation organization at the Harvard Westlake School.

The Venture Vortex

Home to room KC201, the Venture Vortex is a working space for new student ventures at Harvard Westlake. The Vortex is a place where teams come to collaborate and give feedback on each others' projects. Alumni and mentors also visit to give advice and suggestions on student projects.

Learn More

HW Inc.

HW Inc is a five day summer program for motivated HW students to explore business and entrepreneurship. Inc participants can expect to develop valuable business skills, go behind the scenes of a diverse group of companies and ventures, and learn to speak with industry standouts.

Learn More

Lunch and Lecture Series

In collaboration with The Buisness of Life class at Harvard Westlake, the Lunch and Lecuture is an oppertunity for alumni and parents in buisness and innovation come to speak and have lunch with Harvard Westlake students during the school day.
